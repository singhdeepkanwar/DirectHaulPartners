Our Story: From Admiration to Action

As avid followers of international D2C trends, we saw a disconnect. On one side, innovative brands with incredible products. On the other, a massive, digitally-savvy Indian market eager for those products. In the middle was a wall of high shipping costs, long delivery times, and regulatory confusion.

We didn't just want to admire these brands from afar; we wanted to bring them home. DirectHaul was founded to tear down that wall. We built the physical infrastructure, the technological backbone, and the local expertise needed to make selling in India as simple as selling in your home market.

Based in the strategic heart of Punjab, we are more than a logistics company—we are enablers of growth, partners in ambition, and your dedicated team on the ground in India.
